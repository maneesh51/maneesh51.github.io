## About GACM

The German Association for Computational Mechanics (GACM) organizes regular colloquia that bring together researchers in computational mechanics from academia and industry. The GACM Colloquia serve as a platform for presenting cutting-edge research and fostering collaborations.

## Talk Information

Keynote presentation at the **Data Driven Dynamics** minisymposia, focusing on machine learning approaches for dynamical systems and computational mechanics.
