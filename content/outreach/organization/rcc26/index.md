The **Reservoir Computing Conference 2026 (RCC26)** will be hosted at **TU Berlin**, organized by the Chair of Cyber-Physical Systems in Mechanical Engineering (CPSME).

## Conference Overview

Reservoir Computing Conference brings together researchers working on various aspects of reservoir computing, including:

- Echo State Networks
- Liquid State Machines
- Physical Reservoir Computing
- Next-generation reservoir computing approaches
- Applications in time series prediction, classification, and control

## Organizing Committee

- **Chair of CPSME, TU Berlin**
- **Dr. Manish Yadav** (Co-organizer)
